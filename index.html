I'm a junior UX/UI designer with a background in graphic design. I enjoy solving complex problems while finding opportunities to create moments of calm & delight with design.

MY UX SKILLS

User Research

I prioritize empathizing with the needs, preferences, and behaviors of target audiences. Through various methodologies such as interviews, surveys, and usability testing, I uncover valuable insights that inform the creation of intuitive and tailored experiences. I ensure that every design decision is grounded in user-centric principles, resulting in impactful and user-friendly solutions.

UI Design

I specialize in harmonizing aesthetics with functionality, ensuring each element is purposefully placed to enhance usability and engagement. Through meticulous attention to detail and a user-centric approach, I deliver compelling user interface designs that empower users to effortlessly interact with digital platforms.

Prototyping

Using a blend of wireframing, interactive mockups, and rapid iteration, I bring concepts to life, allowing stakeholders to visualize and experience the user journey firsthand. I ensure concepts are grounded in practicality and user-centered innovation, ultimately driving informed decision-making and fostering a seamless transition from concept to functional product.
